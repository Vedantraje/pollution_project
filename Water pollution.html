Water Pollution

The contamination of water bodies such as rivers, lakes, etc. due to several contaminants being discharged in them, directly or indirectly, is known as water pollution.
Water pollution has severe and hazardous impacts on our environment. In this article, we will discuss the causes of water pollution.




Main Causes of Water Pollution

  1. Global warming

  2. Deforestation

  3. Industry, agriculture and livestock farming

  4. Rubbish and faecal water dumping

  5. Maritime traffic

  6. Fuel spillages
